About me

Hey there!
My name is Christoph, and I'm an agricultural economist.
I'm building farm-scale models and data-science tools at the University of Bonn .

On this site I blog about my adventures in the broader field of agricultural sciences and (research) software development.

Most of the topics I enjoy working with involve some kind of data visualisation, websites and web-programming, algebraic modeling or a combination of these. I'm a JavaScript / Node.js enthusiast, which is my go-to language for most of my projects. When writing algebraic models, I'm a huge fan of GAMS , for which I build an Integrated Development Environment (see my projects ). In my spare time, I like to tinker with the Fruchtfolge project(s), as well as GPS based farm documentation and other niceties somewhat related to software and agriculture.
